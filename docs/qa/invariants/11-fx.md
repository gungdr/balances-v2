# Zone: FX

The multi-currency conversion layer the report engine applies before any
net-worth or income figure is summed — `fxConverter` in
`backend/internal/repo/monthly_reports_engine.go`. Every monetary amount is
booked in its **native** currency on the snapshot/transaction and converted to
the household's **reporting** currency at the report month's rate before
aggregation. This zone sits beneath FINANCE the way COST-BASIS does: the
engine-level net-worth assertions (INV-FINANCE-15..17) own the rolled-up number,
while these rows pin the converter's **own contract** at the unit layer, so a
regression that does not move a headline figure — wrong `ok`/rate semantics, the
`foreign()` audit gate firing on the reporting currency — still trips a test. The
manual `fx_rates` table holds *reporting-currency units per 1 unit of a native
currency* per `year_month` (CRUD in `internal/fxrates`, tenancy owned by
INV-TENANCY-11). The defining risk is a **wrong or stale rate silently changing
the headline number**: a future rate leaking backward, a missing rate treated as
zero (or, worse, 1:1) instead of surfaced, or the reporting-currency passthrough
applying a rate it shouldn't. Code: `fxConverter.convert` / `foreign` /
`latestAtOrBefore` in `monthly_reports_engine.go`; tests in
`internal/repo/fx_converter_test.go` (unit) and `monthly_reports_engine_test.go`
(engine-level, under FINANCE). ADR-0002 (multi-currency), ADR-0006
(carry-forward).

| ID | Invariant | Source | Severity |
|----|-----------|--------|----------|
| INV-FX-01 | Passthrough: `convert` returns the amount unchanged with `rate=1` and `ok=true`, consulting **no rate at all**, when the currency is empty, equals the reporting currency, or multi-currency is off. A rate present in the table for that currency must not leak into any of these branches — the single-currency / native-is-reporting path can never fail a conversion | ADR-0002 | High |
| INV-FX-02 | Carry-forward: a foreign amount converts at native × the **latest manual rate at or before its month** (`latestAtOrBefore`), never a future one. With rates at two months, a month between them takes the earlier rate; a month with no later rate carries the last known one forward — the rate is never interpolated or pulled forward from the future | ADR-0006 | Critical |
| INV-FX-03 | Missing surfaced, not zeroed: a foreign amount with no rate at or before its month is unconvertible — `convert` returns `ok=false` and zeroes its outputs, so the engine excludes the position/flow and records a `missingFxEntry` rather than counting it. The amount is **never returned at face value (1:1)**, which would silently misstate net worth | ADR-0002 | Critical |
| INV-FX-04 | Audit gate: `foreign()` is true exactly when multi-currency is on and the currency is neither empty nor the reporting currency. This predicate gates `recordRate`, so `fx_rates_used` carries an audit row for — and only for — a genuinely converted currency (no spurious rate-1 row for the reporting currency, no row when multi is off). `fx_rates_used` is the report's frozen "what rates did this report use?" record (ADR-0006), populated only for foreign currencies per ADR-0002's conversion gate | ADR-0002, ADR-0006 | High |
