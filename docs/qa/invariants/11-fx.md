# Zone: FX

> _Seeded next — the multi-currency conversion layer the report engine applies
> before any net-worth or income figure is summed (`fxConverter` in
> `internal/repo/monthly_reports_engine.go`). Every monetary amount is booked in
> its **native** currency on the snapshot/transaction and converted to the
> household's **reporting** currency at the report month's rate before
> aggregation; this zone sits beneath FINANCE the way COST-BASIS does — FINANCE
> assumes the converted number is right. The manual `fx_rates` table holds
> `reporting-currency units per 1 unit of a native currency` per `year_month`
> (CRUD in `internal/fxrates`, tenancy already owned by INV-TENANCY-11). The
> defining risk is a **wrong or stale rate silently changing the headline
> number**: a future rate leaking backward, a missing rate being treated as zero
> instead of surfaced, or the reporting-currency passthrough applying a rate it
> shouldn't. Candidate invariants: (1) passthrough — `convert` returns the amount
> unchanged with rate=1 and ok=true when the currency is empty, equals the
> reporting currency, or multi-currency is off (no rate lookup at all); (2)
> carry-forward — a foreign amount converts at the **latest manual rate at or
> before its month** (`latestAtOrBefore`), never a future one, multiplying
> native × rate; (3) missing-rate is surfaced, not zeroed — a foreign amount with
> no rate at/before its month is unconvertible (ok=false) and recorded as a
> `missingFxEntry` (the offending position-or-flow + currency), excluded from the
> total rather than silently counted as zero; (4) `fxRatesUsed` records the rate
> actually applied per foreign currency for the month — the audit trail of what
> produced the figure. Annotation targets likely already exist in
> `monthly_reports_engine_test.go` and `monthly_reports_input_test.go` (the
> multi-currency report cases); survey those before writing new tests, then fill
> this table when seeding the zone. ADR-0002 (multi-currency), ADR-0006
> (carry-forward)._
