# balances v0.6.0-alpha.1

> Published as the [`v0.6.0-alpha.1`](https://github.com/kerti/balances-v2/releases/tag/v0.6.0-alpha.1)
> GitHub Release (first alpha, hand-written). From `v0.6.0-alpha.2` onward, release notes are
> auto-generated from merged PRs (ADR-0029).

The first alpha of **balances** — a private household net-worth tracker. Record what you own and
owe, snapshot it over time, and see your whole financial picture across currencies.

## What you can do

**Track everything**
- Bank accounts, property, vehicles, investments (stocks, mutual funds, gold, bonds, time deposits),
  loans, receivables, and income — each with a month-by-month history.
- Bring in past balances by importing a spreadsheet.

**See the whole picture**
- A dashboard with your net-worth headline, income breakdown, and a per-person view.
- Multiple currencies shown side by side — no forced conversion.
- Monthly reports.

**Understand your investments**
- Cost basis and unrealised profit/loss, per holding and across the whole portfolio.
- Value-and-cost-over-time charts, plus category and risk-profile breakdowns.
- Honest valuation: gold is marked at its buyback (cash-out) price, and matured or rolled-over
  deposits no longer show phantom gains or losses.

**Organise your way**
- Tag any holding with your own labels — by bank, by goal, by risk — and see totals per tag.
- Filter lists by risk profile and by routine vs. one-off income.

**Made approachable**
- Fully available in **English and Indonesian**.
- Built-in guided tours on every screen explain how each part works.
- Light and dark themes.

**Sign in & share**
- Sign in with Google; invite household members by email.

## Known limitations in this alpha

- Data and schema are **not guaranteed stable** — this is an early alpha; expect resets.
- **PDF export** of monthly reports isn't available yet.
- Hosting/deploy is still being set up; alpha access is limited.
